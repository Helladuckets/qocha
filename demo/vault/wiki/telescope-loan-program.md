# Telescope loan program

Members borrow a scope like a library book: two weeks, one renewal,
return it clean and collimated.

## The fleet

Two 8-inch Dobsonians (the workhorses), three 80mm refractors for
beginners, and one 90mm tabletop that mostly lives with the school
groups. Theo tracks who has what on the corkboard sheet, which keeps
almost working.

## Rules that earned their place

- Dew shields stay with the scope; two came back mildewed without them.
- Eyepieces travel in the case, never in coat pockets.
- A returned scope gets checked out on the bench before it goes back on
  the shelf — collimation drift blamed a Dobsonian for a month of "bad
  optics" that was one bumped mirror clip.

## During the recoat downtime

While the 14-inch is away for the June recoat (see
[[mirror-recoating-decision]]), the two Dobsonians move to the
dome schedule and member loans pause for five weeks. Open nights run on
the Dobsonians; planet season forgives a lot.
